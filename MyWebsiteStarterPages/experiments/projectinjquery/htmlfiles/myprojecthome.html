Airports:

The International Air Transport Association's (IATA) Location Identifier, a unique 3-letter code, is used in aviation to identify mainly locations of airports throughout the world. Enter the IATA code of desired airport and get its details such as latitude, longitude, name of the aiport. BOS stands for Boston in USA, BOM for Mumbai, India, LHR for London in UK. Flights:

Every flight is represented by IATA in 2-letter code. You can check IATA code on your tickets or here. BA stands for British Airways, LH for Lufthansa, AA stands of American Airlines. You can find out flight details such as delay index, cancellation%, ontime performance etc.
